Datamap

On the datamap it is shown which religion is most prominent by country. also it is possible to see the dispersion of a specific religion by clicking the button of that religion in the top. if clicked on these butteon the religion value in the scatter will also be updated. when clicked on a specific country, the dispersion pie chart will be updated and the country will be highlighted in the scatterplot

Pie chart

The pie chart represents the dispersion of religions over the world. updated per country by clicking on the map. if clicked on one of the arcs the map and scatterplot will be updated corresponding to the religion represented by the arc.

scatterplot

The scatterplot visualisezes the percentages of the selected religion against different social values; literacy rate, happiness index and life expectancy. Social standards are updated with the dropdown menu. religions with the buttons above or the pie chart
